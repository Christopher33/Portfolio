OVERVIEW

Creation of a website for LIVE4 ROAD, located in Picardy.

CONCEPT

  After a first successful experience on the legendary 4L trophy race, the Live4 Road association was born. I created from this blue 4L with a white stripe an identity to the site, by taking the color code and by inlay beautiful photos. The site is a 4 L bible, documenting all the work that is done on the fleet of vehicles that makes up the association. You also have the opportunity to follow their past, present and future adventure.

DEVELOPMENT

  For the development of the site, I created a mockup on Adobe Xd and validated this by my client. I used Symfony 5.1 for the Backend part as well as the support of a Maria DB database. The visual rendering goes through twig, with a nice CSS and JS dressing. The site is fully responsive and allows members of the association to update it without my intervention.
